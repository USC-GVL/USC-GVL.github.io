The Geometry, Vision, and Learning (GVL) Laboratory at the University of Southern California (USC) is passionately dedicated to pushing boundaries in the areas of computer vision, machine learning, and robotics.

Our research is currently centered around the nuanced understanding and representation of 3D scenes, the development of foundation models for intelligent robots and vehicles, and the exploration of geometry-inspired deep learning techniques.
